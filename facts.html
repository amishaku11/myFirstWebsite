Dinosaur Facts

Dinosaurs were some of the most amazing creatures to ever walk the Earth! Explore some interesting facts below and submit your favorite dinosaur fact using the form.

  • T-Rex could run up to 20 km/h despite its huge size.
  • Velociraptors had feathers, not scales like in the movies.
  • Brachiosaurus could reach tree leaves 12 meters above the ground.
  • Stegosaurus had a brain the size of a walnut, but a spiked tail for defense.
