I. Jurassic Park (1993)

Directed by Steven Spielberg, Jurassic Park revolutionized cinema with realistic dinosaurs created using groundbreaking CGI. Follow the story of a theme park filled with cloned dinosaurs and the chaos that unfolds when things go wrong.

II. The Lost World: Jurassic Park (1997)

The thrilling sequel takes the adventure to Isla Sorna, where dinosaurs live freely. Packed with suspense, action, and incredible dinosaur scenes.

III. Walking with Dinosaurs (2013)

A visually stunning family film that blends live-action and animation to tell the story of dinosaurs from a young dinosaur's perspective. Educational yet entertaining!

IV. Dino King (2012)

An animated film from South Korea about a young dinosaur overcoming challenges to survive in a dangerous prehistoric world.

V. Ice Age: Dawn of the Dinosaurs (2009)

A fun, animated spin where the Ice Age characters encounter dinosaurs underground. Perfect for kids and family viewing!
